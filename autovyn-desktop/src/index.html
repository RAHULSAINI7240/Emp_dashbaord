Autovyn Desktop

Installable desktop app that keeps tracking in the background

Install it like a normal desktop app, sign in once with your employee credentials, and let it keep running in the tray. You can also have it launch automatically when your computer starts.

Install once Launch on startup Tray and background sync

This app is already connected to your live Autovyn Render backend. Employees only need their login ID and password.
